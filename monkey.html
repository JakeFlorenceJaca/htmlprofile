HOW TO DRAW A MONKEY

Back to gallery

  • Materials You'll Need

    • Pencil/Pen
    • Eracer
    • Paper
    • colors

      • Step 1: Draw the Face Outline

        1. Start with an oval for the monkey's face, and add two small ovals inside it for the eyes.
        2. Draw an oval below the eyes for the nose and mouth area, with two small nostrils.

      • Step 2: Outline the Head and Body

        1. Draw a circle around the face oval to form the head.
        2. Sketch two lines extending from the head for the arms.

      • Step 3: Draw the Arms and Hands

        1. Extend the lines down from the arms to create the shape of the hands, with simple fingers.
        2. Sketch the feet as rounded, flattened shapes at the bottom.

      • Step 4: Add Ears and Legs

        1. Draw two small, rounded ears on either side of the head.
        2. Sketch the legs below the body, with simple feet at the bottom.

      • Step 5: Add Details to the Face

        1. Add a big smile on the monkey's face and small lines on the hands and feet for the fingers and toes.
        2. Draw a line around the mouth area to separate the face from the outer head.

      • Step 6: Color the Monkey

        1. Add brown coloring for the body and a lighter shade for the face, ears, and belly.
        2. This guide will help you create a cute and friendly-looking monkey!
