HOW TO DRAW A ELEPHANT

Back to gallery

  • Materials You'll Need

    • Pencil/Pen
    • Eracer
    • Paper

      • Step 1: Draw the Head and Body Shapes

        1. Start by drawing a large oval for the body.
        2. Add a smaller circle above it for the head. Place this circle slightly overlapping the top of the oval.

      • Step 2: Add the Ears and Trunk

        1. Draw two large, rounded shapes on either side of the head for the elephant's ears.
        2. Sketch a long, curving line from the center of the head circle downward to form the trunk.

      • Step 3: Draw the Legs

        1. Draw two lines below the body to outline the front legs, slightly wider at the top and narrowing towards the bottom.
        2. Sketch the feet as rounded, flattened shapes at the bottom.

      • Step 4: Add Details to the Body and Legs

        1. Refine the outline of the legs and add two more lines for the back legs.
        2. Draw the tail near the rear end of the body, adding a few small lines at the end to represent the tail's tip.

      • Step 5: Draw the Face and Trunk Details

        1. Add two eyes, positioned near the top of the trunk.
        2. Detail the trunk by adding a series of curved lines across it, creating the appearance of wrinkles.
        3. Add some lines on the ears to show folds and texture.

        This simple guide should help you create a realistic-looking elephant!
