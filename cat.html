HOW TO DRAW A CAT

Back to gallery

  • Materials You'll Need

    • Pencil/Pen
    • Eracer
    • Paper
    • coloring materials

      • Step 1: Draw the Head

        1. Start with a small oval shape for the cat's head.

      • Step 2: Add the Body and Tail

        1. Draw a larger oval below the head for the body.
        2. Add a smaller oval off to the side for the tail's base.

      • Step 3: Add Legs and Paws

        1. Draw two small ovals where the cat's legs would go, just under the body oval.
        2. Sketch tiny circles at the bottom for the paws.

      • Step 4: Outline the Cat's Body

        1. Using the circles as a guide, draw a continuous outline from the head down to the body, shaping the legs and paws. Add small pointed ears on top of the head.

      • Step 5: Refine the Body and Tail

        1. Erase the guiding shapes and draw a curved line for the tail.
        2. Define the legs more clearly, adding small details to give it a natural look.

      • Step 6: Add the Face

        1. Draw two eyes, a nose, and a mouth on the cat's face..
        2. Add some small details to the eyes, such as pupils, to make them expressive.

      • Step 7: Add Whiskers and Fur Details

        1. Add whiskers on either side of the cat's face.
        2. Include any small details for fur around the neck and chest area.

      • Step 8: Add Color

        1. Color your cat drawing with shades of orange and stripes, or any other color you like.
