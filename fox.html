HOW TO DRAW A FOX

  • Materials You'll Need

    • Pencil/pen
    • Eracer
    • Paper

      • Step 1: Draw the Head Shape

        1. Start with an oval or a slightly rounded shape for the wolf's head. Keep it broader at the top and narrower toward the chin.

      • Step 2: Add Guideline for Facial Symmetry

        1. Draw a vertical line down the center of the head shape. This will help keep the features symmetrical.
        2. Add a horizontal line across the head, about a third of the way down from the top. This line will guide the placement of the eyes.

      • Step 3: Draw the Ears

        1. Sketch two upright triangular shapes on top of the head for the ears. They should be large and have a slight inward curve at the base.

      • Step 4: Draw the Nose and Mouth

        1. Place a small, rounded triangle at the bottom of the vertical guideline for the nose.
        2. Draw two lines coming down from the nose, curving outward slightly to form the mouth. Add a small line underneath for the chin.

      • Step 5: Draw the Eyes

        1. Place the eyes on the horizontal guideline, slightly above the nose. Wolves have almond-shaped eyes with a sharp, intense look.
        2. Add some small details around the eyes to give them a more expressive, focused look.

      • Step 6: Add Fur Details

        1. Start adding fur texture around the face and ears, using short, curved lines. Wolves have a thick layer of fur around their neck, so add some fluff around the cheeks and chin to emphasize this.

      • Step 7: Finalize the Ears and Add More Details

        1. Detail the inside of the ears with a few fur lines and add depth by drawing another line inside the initial ear shape.
        2. Refine the nose and mouth area, and add any final shading around the eyes to enhance the wolf's expression.

      • Step 8: Add Shading and Fur Texture

        1. Sketch the Hairline: Start the hairline slightly above the forehead. The shape of the hairline depends on the hairstyle.
        2. Draw the Hair: Add the hairstyle of your choice, keeping in mind that hair flows naturally and has volume.
        3. Draw the Neck: Draw two lines from the base of the oval to the shoulders. The neck should be proportional to the head.
