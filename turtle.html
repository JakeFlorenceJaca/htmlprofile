HOW TO DRAW A TURTLE

Back to gallery

  • Materials You'll Need

    • Pencil/Pen
    • Eracer
    • Paper
    • Optional: Colored pencils or markers

      • Step 1: Draw the Head

        1. Start by drawing a simple oval shape for the head.
        2. Add two small circles inside the oval for the eyes.
        3. Draw a curved line for the mouth.

      • Step 2: Draw the Face

        1. Inside the head, draw a smaller oval for the snout.
        2. Add two small circles inside the snout for the nostrils.
        3. Draw a curved line for the mouth.

      • Step 3: Draw the Shell

        1. Draw a large oval shape below the head for the body.
        2. Connect the head and body with a smooth curve.
        3. Inside the body, draw a smaller oval for the shell.

      • Step 4: Draw the Front Leg

        1. Draw a curved line from the front of the body for the front leg.
        2. Add a small oval at the end of the leg for the foot.

      • Step 5: Draw the Other Front Leg

        1. Repeat Step 4 on the other side of the body.

      • Step 6: Draw the Hind Leg

        1. Draw a curved line from the back of the body for the hind leg.
        2. Add a small oval at the end of the leg for the foot.

      • Step 7: Draw the Pattern of the Shell

        1. Draw a series of lines and curves on the shell to create a pattern.
        2. You can add as much or as little detail as you like.

      • Your Desert Tortoise is Now Done!

        • Tips:

        1. Use light pencil strokes at first so you can easily erase any mistakes.
        2. Don't worry about making it perfect. Have fun and experiment with different styles!
        3. You can add color to your turtle using markers or colored pencils.

          4. Additional Tips:

        4. If you want to draw a different type of turtle, you can adjust the shape of the shell and the pattern.
        5. You can also add details like claws, scales, and a tail.
        6. Be creative and have fun!

          7. I hope this helps!
