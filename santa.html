HOW TO DRAW A SANTA CLAUS

Back to gallery

  • Materials You'll Need

    • Pencil/Pen
    • Eracer
    • Paper
    • Optional: Colored pencils or markers

      • Step 1: Basic Shapes

        1. Start by drawing a large circle for Santa's head.
        2. Below it, draw a smaller oval for his body..

      • Step 2: Add the Body

        1. Draw a curved line connecting the head and body to form Santa's neck.
        2. Draw two curved lines extending from the body to form his arms.
        3. Add two smaller circles at the end of each arm for his hands.

      • Step 3: Draw the Legs

        1. Draw two curved lines extending from the lower part of the body to form his legs.
        2. Add two smaller ovals at the end of each leg for his boots.

      • Step 4: Add the Coat

        1. Draw a curved line from the neck to the bottom of the body to form the front of Santa's coat.
        2. Draw two curved lines extending from the shoulders to the bottom of the coat to form the sleeves.
        3. Add a belt around his waist.

      • Step 5: Draw the Face

        1. Draw two circles for Santa's eyes.
        2. Draw a curved line for his nose.
        3. Draw a curved line for his mouth.
        4. Draw a curved line for his beard.

      • Step 6: Draw the Hat

        1. Draw a curved line from the top of his head to form the brim of his hat.
        2. Draw a curved line from the brim to the back of his head to form the top of his hat.
        3. Draw a pompom on top of his hat.

      • Step 7: Add Details

        1. Draw lines on his coat to create the fur trim.
        2. Draw lines on his boots to create the fur trim.
        3. Draw lines on his gloves to create the fur trim.
        4. Draw a buckle on his belt.

      • Step 8: Color (Optional)

        1. Color Santa's coat red.
        2. Color his pants white.
        3. Color his boots black.
        4. Color his hat red and white.
        5. Color his beard white.
        6. Color his skin pink.

      • Additional Tips:

        1. Practice drawing different shapes and lines to improve your skills.
        2. Don't worry about making mistakes. Mistakes can be erased or corrected.
        3. Have fun and experiment with different styles and techniques.
        4. Remember, this is just one way to draw Santa Claus. You can also find other tutorials and resources online to learn different techniques.
